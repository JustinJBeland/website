Particle Swarm Optimization

I started playing around with Mayavi in Python to visualize the Keane's bump function in three dimensions. After setting the searching parameters of the particle swarm, I tracked their position at each iteration (generation) as shown in the .gif below:

I also changed the view angle of the parameter-objective at each iteration to create slighly more comprehensive illustration. Though this simple example is useful for visualization purposes, it is limited to one, two or three dimensions. For higher dimensional problems it becomes less intuitive to visualize the performance of a given optimization algorithm. Despite this limitation, it is interesting to visualize the behaviour of the swarm i three dimensions.

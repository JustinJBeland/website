Justin J. Beland

I am currently a doctoral stream research scientist at the University of Toronto Institute for Aerospace Studies under the supervision of Dr. P. B. Nair. My work mainly focuses on efficient probabilistic approaches to sequential decision making under uncertainty. This site is designed as a portfolio as well as a place to see what I'm up to.

Recent News

December 1, 2017 - Our paper on Bayesian optimization under uncertainty was accepted to the NIPS 2017 workshop for Bayesian optimization for science and engineering.
